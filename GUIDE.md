## Installation and Launching

1.  **Download:** Get the latest version of the launcher from the official source.
2.  **Launch:** Run the launcher executable.

## Authentication

To use most of the launcher's features, you need to be logged in.

1.  Click the "Login via Browser" button on the home page.
2.  Your web browser will open to the Jagprox authentication page.
3.  Log in with your credentials.
4.  You will be redirected back to the launcher, and you should see your username displayed.

To log out, click the "Logout" button.

## Proxy

The core feature of Jagprox is the proxy.

-   **Start Proxy:** On the home page, click the "Launch Proxy" button. You must be logged in to do this. The proxy will start, and you can connect to `localhost:25565` from Minecraft.
-   **Stop Proxy:** When the proxy is running, the button will change to "Stop Proxy". Click it to stop the proxy.

## Player Stats

You can look up Hypixel player stats for various gamemodes.

1.  Navigate to the "Stats" page from the sidebar.
2.  Enter a player's Minecraft username.
3.  Select a gamemode from the dropdown menu.
4.  Click "Search".
5.  The player's stats for that gamemode will be displayed.

## Player Status

Check if a player is online on Hypixel.

1.  Navigate to the "Status" page from the sidebar.
2.  Enter a player's Minecraft username.
3.  Click "Check Status".
4.  The player's online status and current game (if not hidden) will be shown.

## Logs

The launcher provides logs for the proxy and other operations.

-   **View Logs:** Logs are displayed in real-time on the "Logs" page.
-   **Copy Logs:** Click the "Copy Logs" button (the clipboard icon) at the top of the logs page to copy all the logs to your clipboard.

## Settings

The settings page allows you to manage the launcher.

-   **Check for Updates:** Go to the "Settings" page and click "Check for Updates" to see if a new version of the launcher is available.
-   **Current Version:** The settings page also displays the current version of the launcher.
